More about Rainydays 3 Layer Shell Jacket Men

3 Layer Shell Jacket Men is a technical shell jacket for men from Rainydays that offers wind and waterproof protection with taped seams. The fit can be easily adjusted in the waist and sleeve ends to suit your needs. The two-way zipper can be opened both from above and from below if you need a little extra ventilation. The jacket has two side pockets, two chest pockets and an inner pocket so that you can fit all your smaller things that are good to have on hand on the trip.
